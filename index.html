Long Story Short

“The two most important days in your life are the day you are born and the day you find out why.”
- Mark Twain

1993

I was born 24 years ago. My mother was keeping a diary at that time, and here is the entry from that day (This was translated from Russian, so forgive the English translation): "At 4:45 AM our "sister" was born. From the very first gaze I understood, that her name should be very ringing and free like a mountain stream. Like lightning. Like mountains. I think she would probably love mountains...". My mum decided to call me Aleksandra. It's really weird, but in Ukraine the short name for Aleksandra is Sasha. Yes, I know, nothing in common. :)

1998-2009

Since childhood, I was more interested in math. I realized that it was impossible to become a humanist in family full of physicists. I was very fidgety, so I could never sit still. By the time I finished my secondary education, I had to change 4 schools during the process. I realized that I have to be in constant motion to explore, seek and change the things around me. Life intrigues me and so do all the possibilities it bring. Be brave! Be thirsty! Be alive!

2013-2014

It might seem strange, but a political revolution happening in my country became one of the most life-changing moments for me. It changed me, as well as millions of other peoples' lives. It taught me how to be principled, honest and strong. I learned to take care of other people and set priorities for what is important. The Revolution reminded me that the most important thing in life is to maintain our humanity.

2014

After those events I decided that I want to make my country a better place, although I was aware that I had neither the knowledge nor experience. As a real Russian spy (don't worry, not a real one), I decided to gain my experience from the most developed country in the world – the United States. While my trip was trying, I get to experience changes, difficulty and challenges which, to this day, has made me stronger.

2014

Six years ago at the end of Skydiving Season, I finally met him.
Two years ago we got married.
In-between there was a lot of: breakups, long distance, first kisses and other unforgettable moments.
True love conquers all and with it, we overcame all trials.

2017

From the first "Hello World" that appeared on my screen I was so inspired and so impressed that I felt like I was the creator of a new world. Of course, this was just a beginning. I can't count on one hand how many times my husband has seen me talking to my computer and jumping to the ceiling from happiness after a few hours of figuring out how to solve a function. When I finally made a complete website by myself from scratch, I realized that nothing was in vain.
